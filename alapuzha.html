About hotel

A picturesque 1.5 hour journey away from Kochi lies Seclude Marari Sands. This lush green resort has 13 rooms with wide windows and a classic front porch for a wonderful view of the flourishing outdoors. There are 9 private one-bedroom villas, each designed around a unique element of Kerala to add a splash of vibrance to your stay. Four aesthetic rooms face a clear pool which reflects the bright blue skies.

Meandering stone paths will lead you to an open-air dining where you can relish authentic Kerala cuisine to the music of the waves. It is an experience like no other when you sit on what was once a fishing boat, now a majestic dining table. A few steps beyond that, a sandy beach beckons.

On the outskirts of Allepey, Marari is an oasis of tranquillity for all those who wish to take a break from the hustle bustle of city life.

Seclude Kerala Marari Sands is a property with access to the beach. Most of the events that take place here have all been outdoor or on the beach.
